About

Hi! My name is Nicole, and I am newly planted in central Ohio. Though currently working in healthcare as a Speech-Language Pathologist, I have always had a love affair with webdesign, graphic design, photography and coding. Having started HTML camp at age 10 (yes, that was a thing), I've always enjoyed the creativity and functionality of a beautifully designed website or software application. My dream career would somehow meld healthcare and computer science. Outside of healthcare and computers, I enjoy being outdoors - biking, fishing, running, hiking and playing with our two Shelties are some of my favorite things to do! I am also a sucker for filmmaking, a good book, and have recently delved more into the world of cooking (a gas oven will change your life!). Ultimately, I am someone who loves to perpetually learn, grow, and apply the skills I've acquired to help better the world around me - whether that be with work, family, friends or church. Thanks for stopping by!
